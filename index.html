About me

I am a third year PhD student in the Realistic Graphics Lab at EPFL, supervised by Wenzel Jakob. Prior to joining EPFL, I studied at École polytechnique and Télécom Paris. As part of my Master studies, I interned in George Drettakis's team at Inria Sophia-Antipolis, where I worked on Image-Based Rendering.

My research currently focuses on designing robust differentiable rendering algorithms to solve challenging inverse problems involving light transport.
